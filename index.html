STED Certification

A "STED certificate" refers to a certificate issued by the STED COUNCIL, a national-level autonomous organization in India. These certificates are awarded upon completion of various job-oriented courses in areas like IT, technical skills, and management. The STED COUNCIL aims to promote skill development and self-employment opportunities, particularly for those facing unemployment
